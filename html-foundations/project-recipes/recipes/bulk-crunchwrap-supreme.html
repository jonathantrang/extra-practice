Bulk Crunchwrap Supreme

Prep Time: 3 Minutes

Cook Time: 15 Minutes

Macros

Calories: 1385

Protein: 110g

Carbs: 97g

Fat: 62g

Description

A juicy, delicious, heavy, and macro-friendly take on the traditional Taco Bell clasic.

Ingredients

  • "Mission" High Protein Tortilla - 2 Whole
  • 95% Lean Ground Beef - 10oz
  • Low-Fat Cheese - 50g
  • Fat-Free Greek Yogurt - 125g
  • Tortilla Chips - 10 Whole
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Prep

  1. Season 10oz of 95% Lean Ground Beef with Salt and Pepper or your favorite seasoning.
  2. Crunch up 10 whole Tortilla Chips
  3. Chop up your desired amound of Lettuce
  4. Chop up your desired amount of Tomato
  5. Heat one pan on high heat
  6. Slap some non-stick-spray on the pan.

Crafting the Crispy Cheese

  1. Spread 50g of Low Fat Cheese onto a baking sheet in a circular fashion and put in microwave for 2 minutes.
  2. Let the crispy cheese cool for a bit before adding to the rest of the recipe.

Cooking the Beef

  1. Place beef into the pan and keep stirring/cutting beef into mini bits until beef is cooked.
  2. Check to see if beef is fully cooked by making sure all bits are browned.

Assembling the Crunch Wrap

  1. Add all of your created ingredients onto the middle of 2 whole "Mission" High Protein Tortilla in this order:
    1. Beef
    2. 125g Fat-Free Greek Yogurt
    3. Tortilla Chips
    4. Lettuce
    5. Tomato
    6. Crispy Cheese
  2. Wrap by folding the edges alongside the seam of the middle ingredients by using a pinch-fold method.
  3. Grill both sides of the assembled crunch wrap to seal the seams.
