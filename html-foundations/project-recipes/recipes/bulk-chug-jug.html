Bulk Chug Jug

Prep Time: 5 Minutes

Description

A giant protein shake that's easy to down and makes hitting your macros easier.

Macros

Calories: 2000

Protein: 140g

Carbs: 220g

Fat: 64g

Ingredients

  • Milk - 4 cups
  • Protein Powder - 3 scoops
  • Bananas - 3 whole
  • Oats - 1 cup
  • Peanut Butter - 100g
  • Ice - 1-2 cup
  • Zero Calorie Sweetner

Prep

  • Grab a blender and blend together:
    • 4 cups of 1% Milk
    • 3 scoops of your favorite Protein Powder
    • 1 cup of Oats
    • 3 whole Banana
    • 100g of Peanut Butter
    • 1-2 cup of Ice
    • Desired amount of Zero Calorie Sweetner
