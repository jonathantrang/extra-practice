Lean Chug Jug

Prep Time: 5 Minutes

Description

A giant protein shake that's easy to down and makes hitting your macros easier.

Macros

Calories: 1000

Protein: 70g

Carbs: 110g

Fat: 32g

Ingredients

  • Milk - 2 cups
  • Protein Powder - 1.5 scoops
  • Bananas - 1.5 whole
  • Oats - 0.5 cup
  • Peanut Butter - 50g
  • Ice - 1 cup
  • Zero Calorie Sweetner

Prep

  • Grab a blender and blend together:
    • 2 cups of 1% Milk
    • 1.5 scoops of your favorite Protein Powder
    • 0.5 cup of Oats
    • 1.5 whole Banana
    • 50g of Peanut Butter
    • 1 cup of Ice
    • Desired amount of Zero Calorie Sweetner
